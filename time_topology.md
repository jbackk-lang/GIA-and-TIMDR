# Time Topology (TIMDR)

## 1. Time as an operator
Time is not a dimension or an axis.  
Time is an operation: a trace of information created by projecting twist onto process.

**Time = information trace = projection of twist onto process.**

## 2. Möbius structure of time
The one‑sidedness of time arises from Möbius topology:  
the twist of process creates a direction that cannot be reversed without breaking continuity.

## 3. Double Möbius (M²)
A double Möbius is a twist of a twist.  
It produces:
- interference,

- a central field point,
- a relational grid,
- stabilization of information.

This is physical time in TIMDR.

## 4. Locality of time
Each point of the field has its own local time determined by its local twist and relational structure.
