# TIMDR Glossary (EN/PL)

## Topology / Topologia

| Term | EN | PL | Layer |
|------|----|----|-------|
| Torus | A surface with two independent cyclic directions (u, v) | Powierzchnia z dwoma niezaleznymi cyklami (u, v) | T |
| Mobius Band | A non-orientable surface with a half-twist | Nieorientowalna powierzchnia z polobrotem | T/M |
| Transition Region | Boundary zone between topological modes | Strefa przejsciowa miedzy modalnosciami | T |

## Information / Informacja

| Term | EN | PL | Layer |
|------|----|----|-------|
| Informational Gradient | Change in configuration across the structure | Zmiana konfiguracji wzdluz struktury | I |
| Informational Cycle | A stable repeating pattern | Stabilny powtarzalny wzorzec | I |
| Informational Flow | Propagation of influence through topology | Propagacja wplywu przez topologie | I |

## Modal Dynamics / Dynamika Modalna

| Term | EN | PL | Layer |
|------|----|----|-------|
| Frequency (f) | Rate of periodic repetition | Tempo powtarzania cyklu | M |
| Phase (phi) | Alignment between modes | Wyrownanie miedzy modalnosciami | M |
| Amplitude (A) | Intensity of the mode | Intensywnosc modalnosci | M |
| Coupling | Strength of interaction between modes | Sila sprzezenia miedzy modalnosciami | M |

## Interference / Interferencja

| Term | EN | PL | Layer |
|------|----|----|-------|
| Interference Pattern | Result of overlapping waves | Wynik nakladania fal | I(t) |
| Node | Point of destructive interference | Punkt interferencji destruktywnej | I(t) |
| Antinode | Point of constructive interference | Punkt interferencji konstruktywnej | I(t) |

## Resonance / Rezonans

| Term | EN | PL | Layer |
|------|----|----|-------|
| Resonance Condition | Alignment of frequencies and phases | Wyrownanie czestotliwosci i faz | R |
| Resonant Structure | Stable emergent configuration | Stabilna konfiguracja emergentna | R |

## Emergence / Emergencja

| Term | EN | PL | Layer |
|------|----|----|-------|
| Emergent Field | Coherent distribution created by resonance | Koherentny rozklad powstaly z rezonansu | E |
| Structural Stability | Persistence of a resonant configuration | Trwalosc konfiguracji rezonansowej | E |
| Energy Distribution | Modal amplitude and density pattern | Wzorzec amplitudy i gestosci modalnej | E |
